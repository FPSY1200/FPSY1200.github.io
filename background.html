Who are you?

Hello, I am a simple programmer trying to begin their step into the professional field. I had a lot of good in my programming career thus far, from goofing off with the terminal to exposing myself with industry standard programs.


Let's take a look at the my timeline together, shall we?


2022

I first started learning about programming in my school's Computer Science Principles class. We mainly learned about networking, played with web design and even made prototype apps.


2023

Became president of our high school's computer club, where we competed in competitions like Amazon Voice is Power and Lockheed Martin.


2024

Unfortunately, my technology teacher was unable to continue teaching in my senior year. Our school has dropped all technology courses after her departure.
Despite the lack of programming in school, our club still persists thanks to our computer-nerd digital media teacher Mr. Dennis. We focused on more hardware related things like:

  • Modding 3Ds
  • Computer Tear-downs
  • Introduction to Raspberry Pis
I also started to focus on personal side projects such as:
  • Making my first Roblox Project
  • Remaking my old text adventure game using RenPy
  • Working towards certifications like CS50x Intro to Computer Science
